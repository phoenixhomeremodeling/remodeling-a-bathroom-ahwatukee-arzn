Frequently Asked Questions

How much should a tub to shower conversion cost?

The cost of converting a bathtub to a shower as part of a bathroom remodel typically spans from $1,200 to $8,000, averaging around $3,000, and is influenced by factors such as size, accessibility features, fixtures, installation, and other related aspects.

How do I choose a bathroom renovation?

Prior to commencing significant renovations, it is crucial to contemplate factors including budget allocation, desired return on investment, material quality, bathroom size, and the choice between DIY or hiring a contractor, all of which will influence your decision-making process.

What color bathroom increases home value?

The choice of a light blue or periwinkle bathroom color scheme has the potential to increase a home's resale value significantly, by as much as $5,440 according to a study, whereas an off-white or eggshell bathroom can potentially decrease the value by several thousand dollars.

How much does it cost to tile a shower?

The cost of tiling a shower, encompassing tiles, installation, and related materials, generally amounts to approximately $25 per square foot, resulting in an average total cost ranging from $450 to $1,000, with the final expense contingent on the tile selection and the shower's dimensions.
